FRQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



1. Can I register but not take accommodation?

The registration remains unchanged in both cases, but it is advised to take accommodation as there are no other facilities.


2. Will all the team participants be given accommodation at same place?

We can try but cannot promise for that.


3. Shall I bring my ID card with me?

It is mandatory to bring your college ID card with you.


4. What are the food facilities inside the campus?

For participants other from Poornima Group a food court will be set-up.


5. How will I get to know about the venue of any event.

An iternary will be placed at the entrance gate of each campus with its venue and timings.





For any other Query mail it to aarohan@poornima.org

About Us

Poornima Group of Colleges is proud to present its Annual Inter-College Technical, Cultural and Sports Fest “AAROHAN-2020”.


AAROHAN is in its’ Eleventh Edition of being organized since 2009. A colourful extravaganza, Aarohan-2020 is a four-day fest packed with some main events and numerous other informal events. While exhibiting rich crafts along with other side shows, it will witness a brilliant concoction of music, dance and creative arts. It is catering as a platform for showcasing the enthusiasm of budding professionals whom we proudly call Poornimaite. Taking a Quantum jumps, this time its an inter college fest of National Repute.
