TECHNICAL EVENTS



Live Wire (INTRA)

Circuitry

You have to design a circuit by using different components given by us.

Venue(Intra) - AF-02 & AS-06 (EE DEPT), PIET

Reporting Time(Intra) - 09:00 A.M.

Date - 21/01/2020

Rules And Regulations
  1. Teams of three participants only can contest for this event.
  2. There will be total 3 rounds.
  3. Original Schematic diagram and faulty circuit will be given.
  4. Team has to locate the faults successfully within time limit.
  5. The teams will be promoted to the next round that has completed the task with minimum time.
  6. First round will be elimination round with simple circuits from fields of electronics.
  7. Difficulty level will be raised as teams moving to the final round
  8. There will be time bonus in final round.
  9. All the components and material required for the circuit will be provided by us.
  10. Power supply and other testing devices like multimeter, CRO (if needed) and other tools will be made available in a shared hall.

Cozmo Clench (INTRA)

Pick And Place

Your team must build a manually controlled bot which can do simple tasks of gripping blocks while overcoming the obstacles and completing the run by earning maximum points in minimum time. The bot can be wired or wireless. In case the participants use a wireless mechanism, they must use a dual frequency remote.

Venue(Intra) - Near First Year Block, PCE

Reporting Time(Intra) - 09:00 A.M.

Date - 21/01/2020

Rules And Regulations
  1. Design a manual bot capable of crossing any type of terrain.
  2. It should be wired.
  3. Only one robot is allowed for a team.
  4. Team size is 4./li>
  5. The time and penalty given by volunteers will be considered.
  6. The participants are solely responsible for the safety of their robots and equipment. The organizers are not responsible for any incidents/accidents caused by participants or their equipment.
  7. The track of arena will be shown on the day of the event.
  8. Decision of judges, event organizers and coordinators shall be treated as final and binding on all and cannot be contested.

Burn Out (NFS) (INTRA & INTER)

Robo Race

For all who believe in setting up the place on fire, here is an experience. Build your bot and come on board as BurnOut is on the door! Burn Out is a roborace competition with all teams against one another wheel on wheel. The robot consuming the least time to completing the trace with all the obstacles generated in between. Fastest and most stable bot wins the title

Venue(Intra & Inter) - Near Provison Store, PCE

Reporting Time(Intra) - 9:00 A.M.

Reporting Time(Inter) - 12:00 P.M.

Date - 22/01/2020(Intra) & 23/01/2020(Inter)

Rules And Regulations

    ROBOT SPECIFICATION

  1. The maximum dimension of the robot can be 25cm*20cm*15cm(l*b*h).
  2. The robot may be wired or wireless.
  3. The length of the wire(for wired bots) should be long enough to cover the whole track and wire should remain slack during the complete return.
  4. Maximum weight must not exceed 3 to 5 kg. The participants will be provided with 220 Volts,50Hz standard AC supply. Participants will have to themselves arrange for adaptor or batteries.
  5. The machines must not be made from Lego parts, or any ready-made kit, if we find such machine it will be disqualified.

Registration fees - 500/- Per Team

Messi World (INTRA & INTER)

Robo Soccer

The knack of inherent technology with sports seems to be an inherent quality among students and so to celebrate this very spirit we hail the event of robot soccer. Here the army of robots will battle to score the maximum number of goals.

Venue(Inter & Intra) - Dome Area, PCE

Reporting Time(Inter & Intra) - 09:00 A.M.

Date - 21/01/2020(Intra) & 23/01/2020(Inter)

Rules And Regulations
  1. Each team have a maximum of 4 members.
  2. A robot can push or hit the ball, it cannot withhold or grab the ball.
  3. Human interference during the game isn’t allowed.
  4. If ball is not between the bots, you cannot strike to the other bot.
  5. The robot should fit inside a box of 30*30*25cm.
  6. Robot should not exceed 5 kgs.
  7. Decision of judges, event organizers and coordinators shall be treated as final and binding on all and cannot be contested.

Codex (INTRA & INTER)

Coding

With problems being of varying difficulty levels, Codefest provides the perfect platform for fresh enthusiasts, as well as the experienced ones, to code together and compete for ultimate glory.

Venue(Inter) - Online Lab, PIET

Reporting Time(Intra & Inter) - 12:00 P.M.

Date - 22/01/2020(Intra) & 23/01/2020(Inter)

Rules And Regulations
  1. During this race, each runner runs 100 meters with a baton in-hand.
